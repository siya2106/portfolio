About Me

My name is Siya, and I am from Roorkee. I completed my schooling at Guru Gyan Sagar School. Currently, I am living in Dehradun and pursuing my third year of Bachelor of Technology at Graphic Era Hill University.

I am proficient in C++, Python, and C, and I have a good understanding of data structures. I have also worked on several projects. I developed a Python-based application that converts text to speech (TTS) and speech to text (STT) to enhance accessibility and automation. Additionally, I developed an interactive game using Python designed to improve social, cognitive, and motor skills in individuals with Autism Spectrum Disorder. At present, I am working on a new project related to operating systems, which involves the concept of deadlock.

I have participated in many hackathons and competitions, winning some of them, and I have also attended several webinars. In my free time, I enjoy contributing to GitHub projects and participating in hackathons. Apart from that, I am interested in traveling and making new friends.

My short-term goal is to secure a job in a reputed company, and my long-term goal is to achieve a prominent position where I can build my career and contribute to the growth of the organization.
